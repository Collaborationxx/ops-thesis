About Us

From Mrs. Jacobe humble beginnings, Mrs. Jacobe was able to buy the store from his former employer late 2013. Mrs. Jacobe is now owning the MJ Jacobe Trading and manage the company with Mr. Jacobe, a sibling and four staffs.
